Hello and welcome to Lilly Lotus Arts!

Lilly Lotus Arts is a small start up of artist who want to share their work and make positive remark on the world. The main organizer of the group is Zoe aka Lilly Lotus though still a new artist Zoe inspirers to experiment and combine worlds of art like technology and fashion. The mission of Lilly Lotus arts is to bring light to people's lives whether it be a simple smile or a big laugh we inspire to make people happy. Our art is nothing but hard work and love! We are a family that hopes to spread our love of the unique things of this world.
