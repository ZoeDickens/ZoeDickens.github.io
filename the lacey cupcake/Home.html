Welcome to Zoe's Cupcakery

What do we specialize in?

We do vegan, gluten free, soy free, nut free, and egg free cupcakes. Not all of our cupcakes are vegan, gluten free, soy free, and nut free, that would be impossible! But all of our cupcakes are peanut and egg free! There are no peanuts in the baking facility. Also be aware that there is a chance of cross contamination. Although we do our best to keep from accidental cross contamination. Our cupcakes are made in a facility with other possible allergens. If you are highly allergic remember that this is not an allergen free environment. You are eating these amazing cupcakes at your own risk. If you have a specific allergy please let us know so we can do our best to accomodate you. We are a allergy friendly establishment and we do our best to make cupcakes for everyone!

What is our mission?

To bring amazing cupcakes to everyone! We believe that everyone should be able to enjoy a good cupcake even with allergy’s

Can I custom order?

Yes! We do cupcake orders so feel free to tell us what you need and we’ll make it

Gastonia NC but we orders to anything near Charlotte.
